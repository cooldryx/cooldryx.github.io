How to fix Perl locale warning
===
               
                
``export LANGUAGE=en_US.UTF-8``
                  
                   
``export LANG=en_US.UTF-8``


``export LC_ALL=en_US.UTF-8``


``locale-gen en_US.UTF-8`` May need sudo privilege


``dpkg-reconfigure locales`` May need sudo privilege
